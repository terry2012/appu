As a part of initial install, Appu would ask you to login into a few popular web applications belonging to categories such as social networking, email, professional networking, financial service, and e-commerce.

Before proceeding further, please verify that you have accounts on following sites.

In the next few steps, we would request you to login into the previously selected applications in the following list.

  • Facebook
  • Google
  • Linkedin
  • Paypal
  • Amazon
After logging in, Appu will download personal information fields such as name, address, phone number, last-4 digits of the credit card number, last-2 digits of the social security number from these accounts.

Before proceeding further, Appu will delete ALL of your existing cookies. As a side effect of this action, you would be logged out of ALL the sites where you are currently logged in.

To proceed further and allow Appu to delete all the cookies, please click on the checkbox and click the Next button.
